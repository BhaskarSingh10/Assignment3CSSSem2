Discover World of Ferrero Rocher

Welcome to the world of Ferrero Rocher! Discover the joy that these chocolate candies bring.

Fun Facts

  • Ferrero, the company that makes Ferrero Rocher hazelnut candy, also makes Nutella and Tic Tacs.
  • Ferrero stops producing Ferrero Rocher pralines during summer, because they dont want their product to melt.
  • The world record for 'most Ferrero Rochers eaten in one minute' is 9
  • Turkey produces 75% of global hazelnut supply and Ferrero (Nutella & Ferrero Rocher) consumes 25% of the global supply.

Nutritional Information

  • These chocolates are roughly identical in size as a traditional gumball. Two servings of Ferrero Rocher comprise 150 calories.
  • Ferrero Rocher chocolates probably contain refined sugars rather than organic sugars.
  • Although Ferrero Rocher chocolates are not a healthy option, indulging in them occasionally won't harm you.
  • They are low in sodium and cholesterol, which when consumed in excess can easily cause weight gain.
